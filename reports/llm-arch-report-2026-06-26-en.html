Open-Weight LLM Landscape Report

The open-weight LLM landscape has converged on sparse MoE + long-context efficiency: DeepSeek V4 pushes active params to 49B/13B, MiniMax and NVIDIA explore linear/Mamba hybrid attention, and price wars push frontier capability under $2/1M output tokens. This report verifies parameters from official sources and provides cross-model comparison.

Snapshot 2026-06 Generated 2026-06-26 16 models/variants Sources: Tech reports · HF cards · OpenRouter
Summary Roster OpenRouter Deep Dives Capability Synthesis KV Cache Changes Methodology

Executive Summary

  • The DeepSeek V3 template is now the industry standard. 12 of 16 tracked models are MoE.
  • Attention has forked into four competing paths. MLA dominates frontier; GQA remains mainstream; linear and Mamba are challengers.
  • Hybrid (toggleable) reasoning is now the default. Most new models support a think/no-think switch.
  • MTP at inference is the new throughput battleground. MTP-3 delivers 2-3x throughput gains.
  • Price compression continues but is slowing. Frontier models have stabilized around $0.40-$0.60/$1.50-$2.50.
